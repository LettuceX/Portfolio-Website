Project: Echo Chamber

Role: Lead Systems Designer Engine: Unity (C#) Team: 4 People

1. The Design Problem

Players were spamming the "Fireball" ability because it had the highest damage-per-second (DPS), ignoring the melee mechanics entirely. This made combat repetitive and boring.

My Goal: Force players to swap weapons every 5 seconds to maintain optimal damage output.

2. The Solution: Momentum System

I designed a "Momentum" resource bar:

  • Melee Attacks: Generate Momentum.
  • Ranged Attacks: Consume Momentum.
  • Result: If a player spams Fireball, they run out of energy and deal 0 damage until they go back into Melee range.
